Gregg Bellón

gbStudioNYC

I’m a multidisciplinary artist with over 25 years in NY theater as a creator, designer, and performer.
My work exists in the space where live meets digital; ultimately performative but expressively intermedia.
Engaging video as light, as context, as collaborator, I create intermedia art that merges theater and gallery
and cinema spaces to confront issues of transnational and transcultural identity.
My foundation in live performance leads me to value the interactivity of sharing space with an audience,
in the empathy and complicity of the shared experience.
My approach embraces collaboration of ideas, of forms, of selves to manifest that collective experience.
