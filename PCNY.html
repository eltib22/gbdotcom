About PCNY

Production Consolidated is a one-stop shop for all live event and theatrical production needs. We handle everything from technical direction and production management to set, sound, lighting and video design for arena tours, Spiegeltents, puppet shows, awards ceremonies and full-scale play productions. Production Consolidated of NY is a community of multi-disciplinary artists and artisans providing a full-range of production services for theatre, film/video, and event producers via a collaborative, wholistic approach to process. By engaging each individual as an artist-creator, PCNY promotes a diversity of voices and practices that encourages collective accountability for outcomes as well as process.
